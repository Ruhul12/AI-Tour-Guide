AI Personal Tour Guide Overview

INTRODUCTION

Artificial Intelligence is a system which can be described as a smart computer which can interact with proper knowledge based information to its user. Day by day the use of AI is expanding a lot. AI Personal Tour Guide is a concept design of an AI which will help to navigate its user to in proper goal with rich information.

OBJECTIVE

These days many traveler travels lots of countries To explore more but in a new unknown country they are like strangers in mysterious city, And sometimes its very hard to hire a tour guide.


As an AI Personal Tour Guide is a concept design of an AI which will help to navigate its user to reach in proper goal . As it also has the access of different information it will also help to provide many info about that particular location.

MOTIVATION

Day by day we are moving ahead to lead an extra ordinary life which will be totally controlled through AI. Everyday we can see many invention which are properly shifting us in a next level and also there are many possible outcomes which are helping us to
believe that future is near. Also 4 months ago there was a Blog/ Article which was written in Codeforces.com and also in 2011 from researchgate.net there was another paper which really helps us to motivate to work on this project.
